The Long Road

Play the WebGL Build!

October 24, 2022 - December 9, 2022

EECS494: Computer Game Design and Development,  Group of 4 w/ 2 freelancers

Six week project to focus on mimicking a full game development process, from concept or prototype to finished product. We went with a twin-stick shooter with an inventory-based upgrade system. The map is linear, but loot is randomized and the enemies get progressively more difficult. Developed in Unity.

I was responsible for the inventory, item, and combat systems. I also drew all the in-game pixel art sprites except for the spaceship. The credits are available here as a pdf.
