The Legend of Zelda (1986) Dungeon 1 Recreation

Play the WebGL Build!

September 9, 2022 - September 28, 2022

EECS494: Computer Game Design and Development,  Group of 2

Three week project to remake the first dungeon from The Legend of Zelda (1986) in Unity. The assignment's emphasis was on remaking the core mechanics and didn't require complete fidelity, and also to teach us how to work with Unity.

The assignment also challenged groups to design and implement a custom mechanic and an extra level based on it. We added a hammer, a slow but powerful weapon that can also break certain blocks.
