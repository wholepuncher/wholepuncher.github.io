Super Shoppers

March 27, 2021 - April 17, 2021

EECS493: User Interface Development,  Group of 4

This was a three week long project about creating an app to either bring people together or alleviate some aspect of pandemic conditions. My group decided on a multiplayer cooperative game about running a supermarket dealing with COVID-19. Players would have to survive waves of customers and earn score by helping customers out and cleaning up virus particles. A significant part of the grade was based on our UI development process, from storyboarding to implmentation to user testing.

The game itself was made with Phaser 3 while the networking was done with a node.js server using socket.io.

My main responsibilities were the game's main room, the networking, and the question minigame.
