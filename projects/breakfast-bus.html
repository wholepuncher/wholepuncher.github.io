The Breakfast Bus

September 2018 - December 2018

ENGR100-650: Gaming for the Greater Good,  Group of 4

Semester-long first year project with in GameMaker Studio 2. The class was about creating an accessible and educational game for the visually impaired while learning about engineering processes and technical communication. My group decided to do a game about running a business: a bus-themed food truck serving breakfast. Each level involved driving the bus to a designated location and then cooking what customers wanted to order to earn money.

Outside of overall design, I was responsible for developing the driving portion of each level.
